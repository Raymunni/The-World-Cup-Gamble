⚽ How It Works

48 teams. ? players. One winner. Everyone's randomly assigned a mix of teams across every FIFA seed bracket, picks a captain for each, chooses a wildcard team, and locks in four predictions before a ball is kicked. Every goal scored, every round survived, every correct prediction — it all adds to your points total. Highest points at the end takes first prize.

Points come from four sources: match results, captain goals & assists, your wildcard team's doubled points, and correct predictions. Scroll down for the full breakdown.

🔮 Predictions

Submit four predictions before Matchday 1. Each correct answer earns +10 pts.

  • 🏆 Tournament Winner — pick the team that lifts the trophy
  • ⚽ Best Underdog — most goals by a team seeded 25–48
  • 👟 Golden Boot — the tournament's top scorer
  • 💀 Biggest Flop — fewest goals from a top 10 seeded team
How Teams Are Assigned

🎲 Seeded Random Draft

All 48 World Cup teams are split into seed brackets by FIFA ranking, and one team from each bracket is randomly dealt to every player — guaranteeing everyone gets a realistic spread of strong nations and complete underdogs. With 10 players: Seeds 1–10 go out one per player, then 11–20, then 21–30, 31–40, and a partial fifth bracket. So yes — someone ends up with New Zealand. That's the gamble. (Sorry Trenny.)

🔄 Trades

Not happy with your draw? You can trade a team with another player — but only within the same seed bracket, and only before the tournament starts. Both players must agree and confirm in the group chat. No backsies once the whistle blows.

⚖️ If Two Players Finish Level

  • If still tied → prize money is split equally
