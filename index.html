⚽ How It Works

48 teams. ? players. One winner. Everyone's randomly assigned a mix of teams across every FIFA seed bracket, picks a captain for each, chooses a wildcard team, and locks in four predictions before a ball is kicked. Every goal scored, every round survived, every correct prediction — it all adds to your points total. Highest points at the end takes first prize.

Points come from four sources: match results, captain goals & assists, your wildcard team's doubled points, and correct predictions. Scroll down for the full breakdown.

🎲 Seeded Random Draft

All 48 World Cup teams are seeded 1–48 by FIFA ranking — seed 1 is the highest ranked qualifier, seed 48 the lowest. Each player gets one team per seed bracket (seeds 1–12, 13–24, 25–36, 37–48), randomly assigned — guaranteeing everyone gets a realistic spread of strong and weaker nations. With 12 players that's one team per player from each bracket. So yes — someone ends up with New Zealand (seed 48). That's the gamble. (Sorry Trenny.)

🔄 Trades

Not happy with your draw? You can trade any team with any other player — no bracket restrictions. Both players must agree and confirm in the group chat. No backsies once the whistle blows.
