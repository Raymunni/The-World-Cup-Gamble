How Teams Are Assigned

🎲 Seeded Random Draft

All 48 World Cup teams are divided into seeded brackets based on FIFA ranking, with one team from each bracket randomly distributed to each player — guaranteeing everyone gets a fair mix of strong and weaker nations.

For example with 8 players, brackets are groups of 8: Seeds 1–8 are randomly dealt out (one per player), then Seeds 9–16, then 17–24, and so on until all 48 teams are assigned. Each player ends up with 6 teams total. The bracket size scales with however many players join. Trades are allowed within the same bracket before the tournament — both players must confirm in the group chat.

Captain & Wildcard

🧢 Captain

Pick one captain per team. They earn you points for every goal and assist — tracked via Sofascore.

  • +1 pt per goal
  • +1 pt per assist
  • Locked before Matchday 1
  • Penalty shootout goals don't count

🎲 Wildcard Team

Pick one of your teams as your wildcard. All points that team earns are DOUBLED.

  • Cannot be your top-seeded team
  • Match & knockout points DOUBLED
  • Captain bonus points are NOT doubled
  • Locked before Matchday 1
